Arm Workout Movement

The arms are defined by the biceps on the front and the triceps on the back. While the biceps handle elbow flexion (curling), the triceps actually make up about two-thirds of the upper arm's mass and are vital for all lockout pushing movements. Balanced training ensures both aesthetic symmetry and the strength needed for heavy compound exercises.
