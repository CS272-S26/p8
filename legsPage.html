Legs Workout Movement

The legs house the largest and most powerful muscles in the body, including the quads, hamstrings, and glutes. Training lower-body movements like squats and hinges drives metabolic demand and builds a solid "engine" for the rest of the body. Beyond aesthetics, strong legs are the primary drivers of explosive power and long-term joint health.
