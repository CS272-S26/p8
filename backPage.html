Back Workout Movements

The back is a complex network of muscles including the lats, traps, and rhomboids. These muscles are responsible for pulling movements and maintaining an upright, healthy posture. Training often focuses on vertical pulls, like chin-ups, and horizontal rows, which build the "V-taper" look while providing essential stability for the spine and shoulders.
