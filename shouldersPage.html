Shoulder Workout Movements

The shoulders are comprised of three distinct heads: the anterior (front), lateral (side), and posterior (rear) deltoids. Because the shoulder joint has a massive range of motion, training requires a variety of angles, such as overhead presses and lateral raises. Strong deltoids create upper-body width and are crucial for overhead stability and functional lifting.
